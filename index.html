What is Neoffice ?

Neoffice is a startup created in 2021 by four students of IESEG : Le Maire Gildas, Bansept Luc, Paulin Arthur et Delorme Théophile. Our teacher of Business Exploration gave us an exercice about innovation and creating a startup. We wanted to innovate in the office structe : desk, chair, computer, printer, ... We got the idea of creating a the companie Neoffice and to make a prototype of our first product : the SmartDesk.
